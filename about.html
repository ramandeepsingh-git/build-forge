Clean Vanilla Codebase

Architecture Guidelines

  • Decoupled Logic: Business equations (PSU calculator, compatibility engine checks) are completely decoupled from layout renders.
  • Defensive Schema: Missing slots, drafts, or catalog data return fallback states rather than throwing runtime errors.
  • No Bundlers: Raw files ship to browser targets directly, utilizing native modern ES6 imports.
Builder Handbook

Assembly Roadmap

A step-by-step checklist guide to installing and configuring your PC components.

Step 01

Install CPU

Align the golden triangle marking on your processor with the socket corner, place it gently, and secure the socket latch arm.

Step 02

Seat Memory (RAM)

Push open DIMM slot notches, line up the offset RAM connector gap, and press down firmly on both ends until double notches click back.

Step 03

Mount Motherboard

Line up motherboard I/O shield ports with case cutouts, check screw alignment with case metal standoffs, and secure board screws.

Step 04

CPU Cooler

Apply a pea-sized dot of thermal compound paste to the CPU, mount the cooler bracket, secure heatsink screws, and plug fan into CPU_FAN header.

Step 05

Seat GPU (PCIe)

Open the primary PCIe x16 slot lock, remove case rear slot brackets, slide card down until latch clicks, and screw GPU bracket to case frame.

Step 06

Install Storage

Mount M.2 NVMe SSDs into board sockets at a 30-degree angle, lay flat, and screw down. Secure SATA HDDs/SSDs inside case drive mounts.

Step 07

Power Connections

Install PSU into bottom case shroud. Connect main 24-pin Motherboard cable, CPU 8-pin connector, GPU PCIe power pins, and SATA lines.

Step 08

POST and BIOS

Plug case power pins to board front headers, connect HDMI to GPU port, boot system, check board diagnostic LEDs, and enter BIOS setup.
